    -FAQ-

    Frequently Asked Questions:

    Who is included in my invitation?

    In some cases your invitation was delivered without a specific 'deliver-to' address. But your partner/spouse and kids are all included, of course.

    Will you help us getting settled in the area?

    We have provided some info on accommodation and what to do in Cagliari in the dedicated sections of the website. But we are more than happy to help you contact the locals, renting a car or getting in touch with other fellow party-goers whom you might want to share a car or a trip with!

    What weather should we expect?

    At the end of May, thanks to the neighbouring patron fest in Villasalto (which gets all the bad weather), we usually have pleasant warm days and cool nights. The days might be around 25˚C, but it gets chilly in the evenings. Mosquitos shouldn't be too much of an issue yet, but we'll provide some repellers.

    Is this a good time for a holiday in Sardinia?

    As we said, the weather should be very good for holidays. Just remember: it's going to be holiday season in most of your countries (Ascension/Bank holiday) and here (national holiday on June 2nd), so don't forget to book a bit in advance if you don't want to have trouble finding a good deal.
